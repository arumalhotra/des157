What's in my bag?

⊗

As a designer, I always need to have some form of book in my bag. You never know when creativity would strike, and I would be the next billionaire!

⊗

If I carry my bag, I always carry my laptop. For my career and school I pretty much need my laptop all the time!

⊗

I don’t like talking to people while I am on the bus, and I catch up on my tv shows while traveling. Hence, I always carry my earphones.
